About Me

Welcome to my first web-page that I have had the great pleasure of creating. I hope you enjoy the design I chose in an attempt to convey a little bit of professionalism to you, the viewer. Enough about the design and more about me, shall we. I am a typical thirty year old man, ambiguous about my future but always hopeful and eager to achieve something better than what I have. I am a very energetic person. In my free time I like to play racquetball, practice Brazillian Jiu-Jitsu, run, code and play video games to name a few.


But all of that fun stuff was in the past. Coding Bootcamp, taught by the wonderful people at Trilogy and hosted by UPenn, is the rest of the story now. Creating magnificent pieces of art such as the one in view right now is where all this artists time goes to. I dont want to come off as sarcastic because it is a labor of love. Not unlike the struggle to earn another stripe on your belt, it takes hard work and patience to be great. Live and learn, invest your time and money into health and education and life will work itself out.
